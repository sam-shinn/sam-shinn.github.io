Traditional Retirement Plans Still Carry Market Risk

Many retirement accounts rely heavily on market performance. That can create stress when volatility hits, especially near or early in retirement.

Sequence of return risk can be especially damaging: if large losses happen while you are taking withdrawals, your portfolio may have less time and less capital to recover.

A Protected Alternative

Guaranteed growth IRA strategies are designed to help protect principal from market losses while giving you the opportunity to earn returns tied to major market indexes like the S&P 500 and Nasdaq.

Some strategies offer uncapped index-linked crediting methods, which means your money can participate in market upside without being directly invested in the market.

Why These Strategies Are Disruptive

These strategies can be disruptive because many have little to no direct fees for clients.

As a result, some advisors may not talk about them because they do not typically profit from the same high ongoing asset-management fees charged on traditional portfolios.
