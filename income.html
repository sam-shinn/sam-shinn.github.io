The Traditional 4% Rule May Not Be Enough

A $500,000 traditional IRA using the common 4% safe withdrawal rule may produce about $20,000 per year with no lifetime income guarantee and continued exposure to market losses.

For many retirees, that creates a difficult question: What happens if the market drops early in retirement or you live longer than expected?

The good news is, large financial services institutions are now offering an alternative - Safe retirement income strategies that generate an 8% yield or higher guaranteed for life while keeping your retirement completely shielded from market risk.

A Different Way to Create Retirement Income

Guaranteed income IRA strategies can often create lifetime income payout rates of 8% or higher, depending on age, product, and carrier terms.

Some strategies may also offer guaranteed growth rates as high as 10% on the income benefit base while your principal remains protected from market loss.

Simple goal: convert a portion of retirement assets into a protected income stream you cannot outlive.

Why Many People Never Hear About These Strategies

These guaranteed income strategies can be disruptive in the financial services industry because many have little to no direct fees for clients.

That also means some advisors may not prioritize them because they do not typically create the same high ongoing asset-management fees as traditional portfolio management.
